WILD anchor

Instrukcja
1. Go to sleep and wake up after 4-6 hours
2. Wake up for 30min - 1 hour, here you can set up program
3. Go back to bed
4. When signal beeps focus on it, after this try to fall asleep, repeat until you'll eventually enter the dream.
Good luck!

Settings
Interval - what is time between every beep
Beep length - length of the beep 
Expire time - after what time aplication should turn off


Time is in seconds
